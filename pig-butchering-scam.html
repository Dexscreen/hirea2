What is Pig Butchering Scam?

Pig butchering scam is a sophisticated long-term fraud where scammers build romantic or friendship relationships with victims before convincing them to invest in fake cryptocurrency platforms. The name comes from the process of "fattening up" victims before "slaughtering" them financially.

How Pig Butchering Scams Work

Understanding the pig butchering process helps victims recognize they've been scammed and take action:

Stage 1: Initial Contact

Scammers contact victims through dating apps, social media, or "wrong number" texts, appearing friendly and interested in building a relationship.

Stage 2: Building Trust

Over weeks or months, scammers develop emotional connections, sharing fake personal stories and photos to gain the victim's trust.

Stage 3: Investment Introduction

Once trust is established, scammers casually mention their success with cryptocurrency trading and suggest the victim try it.

Stage 4: The Hook

Victims are directed to fake trading platforms that show impressive returns on small initial investments, encouraging larger deposits.

Stage 5: The Slaughter

After victims invest significant amounts, withdrawal requests are denied with various excuses, and the scammer disappears.

Pig Butchering Scam FAQ

Can I recover money from pig butchering scams?

Recovery is possible but challenging. Success depends on how quickly you act, the methods used by scammers, and whether funds are still accessible. Our experts maximize recovery chances.

How long do pig butchering scams typically last?

Pig butchering scams can last anywhere from weeks to over a year. Scammers invest significant time building trust before requesting money.

Why is it called pig butchering?

The term comes from the process of "fattening up" pigs before slaughter. Scammers build relationships and trust (fattening) before stealing money (slaughter).

How can I verify if a trading platform is legitimate?

Check regulatory databases, look for proper licensing, verify company registration, and be wary of platforms recommended by online contacts.
