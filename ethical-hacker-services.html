What is an Ethical Hacker?

An ethical hacker is a cybersecurity professional who uses the same techniques as malicious hackers but with permission and for legitimate purposes. Our certified ethical hackers help organizations identify and fix security vulnerabilities before they can be exploited by cybercriminals.

Ethical Hacker FAQ

What is the difference between ethical and malicious hacking?

Ethical hackers work with permission to improve security, while malicious hackers operate without authorization to cause harm or steal data.

How often should I hire an ethical hacker?

We recommend annual penetration testing, with additional testing after major system changes or security incidents.

Will ethical hacking disrupt my business operations?

Our ethical hackers work carefully to minimize disruption, often conducting tests during off-hours or in controlled environments.

What happens after the ethical hacking assessment?

You receive a detailed report with findings, risk ratings, and specific remediation recommendations to improve your security posture.
